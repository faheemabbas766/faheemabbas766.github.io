Ball Sort

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Ball Sort handles information when you install and play the game.

Effective date: June 19, 2026

Package name: com.rexwise.colorsort

Information We Collect

Ball Sort does not require you to create an account and does not ask you to submit your name, email address, phone number, password, or payment details inside the app.

The app may process limited information through Google services and locally on your device, including:

  • Gameplay progress such as levels, coins, themes, settings, and daily challenge status.
  • App interactions and usage events for analytics.
  • Crash logs, diagnostics, device type, operating system version, and app version.
  • Advertising identifiers and approximate device information used by Google AdMob to show and measure ads.

How We Use Information

Information is used to:

  • Save your game progress and preferences on your device.
  • Provide daily challenges, rewards, themes, and gameplay features.
  • Show banner, interstitial, and rewarded ads.
  • Understand app performance and improve the game experience.
  • Detect and fix crashes, bugs, and technical issues.

Advertising

Ball Sort uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use information such as advertising identifiers, device information, app interactions, and approximate location to provide, limit, measure, and improve advertising. Rewarded ads are optional and are shown only when you choose to watch an ad for an in-game reward, such as an extra tube.

Analytics and Crash Reporting

Ball Sort uses Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage and Firebase Crashlytics to collect crash reports and diagnostics. These services help us improve stability and gameplay quality.

Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google AdMob
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google Play In-App Review and In-App Update services

These services are provided by Google and may process data according to Google's own privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

Ball Sort is a casual puzzle game and is not designed to knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us so we can review and take appropriate action.

Data Storage

Gameplay progress and settings are stored locally on your device. If you uninstall the app or clear app data, local progress may be removed. Analytics, advertising, and crash data may be processed by Google services according to their retention policies.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Limited app, device, advertising, analytics, and diagnostics data may be processed by Google services listed above to provide ads, analytics, crash reporting, and Play Store features.

Your Choices

  • You can reset or limit your advertising ID in your Android device settings.
  • You can disable personalized ads through your Google account or Android ad settings where available.
  • You can clear local game progress by clearing app data or uninstalling the app.
  • You can contact us to ask questions about privacy or request data-related assistance.

No Gambling or Cash Rewards

Ball Sort does not include gambling, betting, wallet features, cash-out, redeemable cash rewards, or real-money prize systems. In-game coins and rewards have no real-world monetary value.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact
