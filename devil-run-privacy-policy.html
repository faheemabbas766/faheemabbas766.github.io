Devil Run Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 11, 2026

Rexwise (“we”, “our”, or “us”) provides Devil Run as a free mobile game. This policy explains information processed when you use the game.

Information we process

Devil Run does not require an account and we do not directly collect your name, email address, contacts, precise location, photos, or messages. Game progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

The game uses Google AdMob to display optional rewarded ads and limited ads at natural game breaks. Subject to your consent and regional requirements, Google and its advertising partners may process device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, approximate location, ad interactions, diagnostics, and other device or usage information to deliver, limit, measure, and protect ads.

Devil Run also uses Google Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. These services may process app interactions, device identifiers, diagnostics, crash logs, and related technical information so we can understand gameplay, improve performance, and fix crashes.

Consent and choices

Where required, Devil Run displays Google’s consent form before requesting ads. You can review or change applicable advertising privacy choices through the Privacy Options control in the game settings. You can also reset or limit the advertising identifier in your Android device settings.

Third-party services

Google AdMob and Firebase process data under Google’s policies. Learn more at Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from apps that use its services.

Data retention and security

Local game progress remains on your device until you clear app data or uninstall the game. Third-party providers retain information according to their own policies. We use reasonable safeguards but no electronic system can guarantee absolute security.

Children

Devil Run is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing of personal information. For advertising data controlled by Google, use Google’s privacy controls. You may contact us for other privacy requests.

Changes

We may update this policy as the game or legal requirements change. The latest version and effective date will remain available on this page.

Contact

For privacy questions, email contact@rexwise.com.
